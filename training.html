Frequently asked Questions

I am new to programming. How do I know the courses to choose?

As a beginner, you are advised to start with the beginner courses but we also encourage you to speak to us preferably via whatsapp( +234 9027719366 or +234 813853 7312) for proper clarification.

What is the time for the training, and is the training limited to students alone?

it is usually from 4 to 6pm on weekdays and then weekends will be scheduled based on the course and please note the training is not meant for students alone

Where is the training holding?

The training is set to take place at two faculties in the University of Benin, faculty of Engineering and faculty of the Physical Sciences.

Can I combine my academic work with the training?

Yes of course. In life, you need to learn how to multitask because life doesn't present you with one thing at a time, also this moment would be the best time for you because you still have some time to yourself and there is little to non financial pressure on you.

i'm already into programming, which course should I choose?

Choose the one you need most. Some courses although require the knowledge of a previous one. You can reach us via our number for orientation.

How much for the various courses?

some of the courses are at five thousand naira, generally it is based on the course you put in

Will two weeks be enough to go through a course?

Yes, you can learn a whole lot in two weeks but not everything about it. We will train you to a level where you would be able to fly on your own.

How do I pay?

Payment would be at the venue on the first day of the program.

When can I start?

As soon as you make up your mind...Contact us at (+234 9027719366 or +234813 853 7312)

Can I do all the courses?

Yes, you can.

I don't have a laptop yet, can I still partake in the training?

For the beginner courses yes you can code on your mobile device but for the intermediate and advanced courses, please you need a laptop.

Will materials and software be given to the trainees?

Yes, these will be provided at the training center for the courses you registered for. However, for the courses you didn't put in for but you are interested, you would have to pay for the materials and software.
